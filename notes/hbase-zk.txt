Thank you for installing ZooKeeper on your Kubernetes cluster. More information
about ZooKeeper can be found at https://zookeeper.apache.org/doc/current/

Your connection string should look like:
  hbase-zk-zookeeper-0.hbase-zk-zookeeper-headless:2181,hbase-zk-zookeeper-1.hbase-zk-zookeeper-headless:2181,...

You can also use the client service hbase-zk-zookeeper:2181 to connect to an available ZooKeeper server.
